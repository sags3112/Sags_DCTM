What is the maximum length of the password that can be passed in Content Server (CS) 5.2 ?

	
In the 5.2 version of Content Server (CS) the length of the password that can be passed from the client DMCL has been bumped to 8160 characters. The 4.x version of the DMCL / CS was capable of passing passwords less than or equal to 128 characters
